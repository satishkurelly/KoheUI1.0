FORGOT PASSWORD

  1. The password should consist of at least one alphabet and one number.
  2. The password should be a minimum of 7 characters.
  3. Password cannot be same as User Name – spelled forward or backward.
  4. Password cannot be a single character repeated 6 times.
  5. Password cannot have more than two pairs of repeated characters.
  6. The new password must differ from the old password by at least 1 character.
